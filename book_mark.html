第一次接觸金工。

謝謝花鑿的指導和分享。

算是第一次和本地工藝創作者交流，自己因此發白日夢。也許將來我都可以在香港做一個工藝人。也許將來我可以有一間工作室。嗯，也許。

This was a gift for a friend.
It was my first time doing metal work.
花鑿 was a brilliant teacher and an interesting person.
I think it was this experience that planted the idea in me - maybe I could also be a craftman in Hong Kong.

Maybe one day I could have my own studio...
