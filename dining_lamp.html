Dining Lamp 吊燈

送給木工老師的禮物。

2019年到台灣學習木工，短短一個月，算是學習了最基本的基本功。但最珍貴的收穫，不是技術的進步，而是能夠遇上一些美麗的靈魂。

想要送給他們一樣東西，一盞溫柔的燈也許不錯。

材料有，回收的草帽和竹吸管、透光的木和釣魚線。

The dining lamp was a gift for my woodworking teacher's family.
I wanted to make a lamp that could help people relax after a long day a work, so I came up with this design of floating wood pieces, which resembled patches of light emitted from a central light source.

Materials used consisted of a straw hat, a bamboo straw and pieces of translucent scrap wood. The wood pieces were boiled in water, shaped in some pots, and dried over the course of a few days, after which I hung them up piece by piece using fishing lines.
