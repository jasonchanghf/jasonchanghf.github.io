Earphones holder

曾經有一段日子，很害怕浪費時間。時不時會因此突然心跳加速，手心冒汗，焦慮不安。

這個耳機套就是那時候的產物。

「每天節省20秒，一年就會多出121.7分鐘。」

問題是解決了，但問題其實從來不是問題，真正的問題是自身。

現在漸漸學會慢下來。

起床時先攤著，伸個懶腰。

I dreaded wasting time.
There were times when I worked extremely hard non-stop.
There were also times when I just played video games for days on end, drowned in the painful pleasure of oblivion.
Yet, the fear of losing time haunted me every waking hour.

I guess the earphones holder was a product of those days.
"Imagine saving 20 seconds a day. That's 121.7 minutes per year"

Looking back, this product was like a perfectly executed bad joke.
It solved the problem alright, but the problem was never a problem.
The problem was probably within myself.
