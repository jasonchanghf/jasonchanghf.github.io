Friction Arm

This project began last summer. Thought I could finish it in two months but it took much longer than expected.
The project is therefore still on-going. Hopefully the first version can be made this summer!

The idea is to invent a friction arm that is entirely made of wood - and idealy no glue is required. In fact, everything except for the hinge dowels are just laser-cut parts, so it can be made extremely easily.

Why am I trying to make a wooden friction arm when so many metal and plastic friction arms already exist? The simple answer is - I want to make it in wood! I also think this mechanism is better than the classic 'nuts and bolts' mechanism in many ways, be it aesthetics, strength, or ease of use. With some modifications, it can actually turn into something so much more. Anyway, no point talking the talk before walking the walk... so stay tuned!
