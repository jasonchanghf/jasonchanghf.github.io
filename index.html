Friction Arm 木製折臂

斷斷續續用了兩個月的時間，總算雛形已成。之後香港發生了很多事情，自己也經歷了不少磨練，因此花了很多時間反思，比如，何謂善，何謂不善；比如，生於此地，何去何從。

透過今天的雙眼，再審視當初的設計意念，只能說......嗯，都仲幾鍾意，好彩......

設計意念很單純，就是想要設計出一個全木製的伸縮臂，能夠做到幾點：簡樸、實用、自然美。其實很簡單，只是世界太複雜，所以設計不容易。

希望能夠趕快騰出時間，繼續創作。

This project began last summer in MakerBayHK. Thought I could finish it in two months but it took much longer than expected. The project is therefore still on-going. Hopefully the first version can be made as soon as possible.

The idea is to invent a friction arm that is entirely made of wood - and idealy no glue is required. In fact, everything except for the hinge dowels are just laser-cut parts, so it can be made extremely easily.

Why am I trying to make a wooden friction arm when so many metal and plastic friction arms already exist? Ummmm... I guess I just like wood too much.
