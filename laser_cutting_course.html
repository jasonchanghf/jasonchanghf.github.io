Laser Cutting Video Course 雷射切割影片課程

Click here to go to https://makerbay.net/courses/laser-cutting-l1/

在MakerBay實習時的任務之一：製作一個雷射切割的影片課程。資料蒐集、編寫課程、寫稿、錄影、剪接，一腳踢，幸得mentor Maria指導，成品雖不算最滿意，但也可接受。

製作影片比想像中難且耗時，幾乎用了兩個星期去製作25分鐘的影片。第一次剪片，獲益良多，也許有朝一日會大派用場......

One of my main tasks at MakerBay during the internship was to create a video course for laser cutting. I carried out research, wrote the content, typed up the script, filmed and editted the videos...(Luckily with the help of my mentor, Maria) Took me nearly two weeks to create 25 minutes of content. Learnt a lot about video editting - a useful skill to have!
