Tensegrity Tower 張拉整體高塔

大學三年級時候的project。第一次用Rhino和Grasshopper繪圖。「Mindmap繪圖法」，好正！

我總覺得Tensegrity(張拉整體)有一種獨特的美感：剛柔並重，能屈能伸。也許這是因為大自然中恰巧就有很多Tensegrity的結構，所以有一種難得的自然美。希望有機會把這種結構融入自己的創作當中。

This was part of my third-year project in May 2020. As a group of three, we needed to design a tension structure. It was the perfect opportunity because I had always wanted to design a tensegrity structure!

I did most of the Rhino and Grasshopper modelling, which was great fun. Using a mindmap to code was the best thing ever?

Although tensegrity is a very inefficient design just by considering 'the shortest load path', I think it has lots of potential, especially in smaller structures.

There is some elegance in it that I just can't describe. Maybe it is to do with the fact that the nature is largely made of tensegrity, and so tensegrity is closer to the nature?
